## Interview Responses Needed

The research plan and interview questions have been generated. Now it's time to collect actual interview data.

### What to provide

Paste your interview notes, transcripts, or summaries below. You can include:

- **Full transcripts** from recorded interviews
- **Summary notes** from each interview session
- **Key quotes** and observations
- **Participant identifiers** (P1, P2, etc. — no real names needed)

### Tips for better results

- Include responses from at least 3 participants for pattern detection
- Note any non-verbal observations (hesitation, confusion, enthusiasm)
- Include both positive and negative feedback
- Mark any surprising or unexpected responses

### How to respond

Paste all your interview data below. The more detail you provide, the richer the synthesis will be.
